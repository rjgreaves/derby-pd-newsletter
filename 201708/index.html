How Can Derbyshire Carers Association Help You?

Carers often become so focused on the person they care for that they forget to look after themselves. To keep going it is essential that carers look after their own health and well-being and that’s when Derbyshire Carers Association can help.

  • Carer assessments
  • Help with Personal Budgets
  • 1-1 Support
  • Emergency planning
  • Information and advice
  • Local carer support groups
  • Respite opportunities
  • Carers News magazine
  • Training and events
  • Liaison with health professionals and partner organisations. They have a Health and GP liaison project too.

Contact 01332 200002

Free on Monday mornings?

The Creative Challenges Group meet at Aston Court Hotel each Monday, 10.30-12.30. We can all be creative and it seems doing small creative activities can help our well-being.

This newly formed group is keen to welcome new members. The founder, Jo Hunter, has handed over to Ann Pitt, but Jo will pop back to keep in touch regularly. Contact Ann Pitt or Liz Greaves for more information or just turn up!

The group met for the first time on Monday, 19 June, with Ann guiding us through the activities. This week the focus was on words and limericks, which resulted in a lot of creativity and laughter - we are looking forward to being surprised with fresh challenges each week.

Please note that on 24 July the group will meet at Derby Museum for the entire session. The doors will open briefly at 10.30 for us, so we do need to be on time, please, meeting in the Reception area. All challenges will be based around the museum exhibitions and should be most interesting!
