Festival of Trees

St John’s Church on Bridge Street Derby are holding a Christmas Festival of Trees and Parkinson’s UK Derby branch have been asked to display a decorated tree to promote our Branch. Joy Riddle ( who helps at our coffee mornings ) is organising the details with the church.

If anyone has a tree which is under 5 ft tall that we can borrow for the weekend of 1st – 4th December we would be very grateful. We are hoping that people will help decorate stars at either the coffee morning or the branch meeting. Little Eaton Garden Centre have kindly donated lots of material for us to use to decorate the stars and tree. We would also be delighted if anyone could come and help on the 1 December between 10am and 4pm to put up and decorate the tree or indeed help take it down again on the 4 December from 2.30 onwards. This is yet another way to promote the local support for people with Parkinson’s and raise awareness of the condition. We hope you might be able to support us, either by helping or going along over that weekend to see all the trees displayed. More details next month. If you are able to help in any way contact Liz Greaves 01332 515700.

Membership is open to all
What do members get?
  • 4 editions a year of our magazine - Parkinson. Each issue is full of experiences of people living with Parkinson's and current information about the condition.
  • 2 editions a year of our research magazine - Progress Providing updates on the studies we fund and details of how to get more involved with Parkinson's research.
  • Support on your doorstep- Local Branches where you can meet other people living with Parkinson's for support and enjoy many activities
  • Your voice heard. Help shape our future and campaign for a better quality of life for people living with Parkinson's.
