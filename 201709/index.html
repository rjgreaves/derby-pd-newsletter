New Facebook page created for Carers in Derbyshire

A brand new Facebook Page has been launched as an online community and information source for carers in Derbyshire: www.facebook.com/carersinderbyshire

Carers in Derbyshire, a partnership of organisations and family carers in the area, has created the page to keep carers up-to- date with news, events and to give information about local support services. It is also hoped the page will become an online community by allowing carers to communicate with each other and share their experiences. Carers in Derbyshire also has a website that acts as a starting point for local carers who are looking for help, advice and support services online.  There are currently 118,000 unpaid carers in Derbyshire and Derby City, looking after a family member or friend who could not manage without their help.

The Facebook Page was launched on the 2 August 2017 – please ‘like’ the page and join the community.
